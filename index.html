Problem Statement: Campus Map

Students all over the country are entering college without getting the opportunity to have an in-person orientation, and are expected to be able to navigate the campus on their own with no prior knowledge of the campus layout. New students need an easier way to be able to find out where their classes are, and how to get to them without being late to class.

Affinity Diagram: Campus App

As we created this Affinity Diagram, we came up with more ideas to have a lot more usage than just a map. We decided that this app can have much more purpose for the school, instead of just a map.
